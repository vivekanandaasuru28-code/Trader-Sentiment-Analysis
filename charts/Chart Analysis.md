PnL vs Sentiment:
PnL peaks during Extreme Greed periods, indicating that traders benefit most from momentum-driven markets,
while lower returns in Fear phases reflect uncertainty and less efficient trading conditions.

Trade Size vs Sentiment:
Trade sizes increase significantly during Fear periods, suggesting emotionally driven risk-taking, 
where traders deploy larger capital despite weaker performance outcomes.

Win Rate vs Sentiment:
Win rates are highest during Extreme Greed, reinforcing that favorable market sentiment improves trade success, 
while lower win rates during Fear highlight the impact of uncertainty on decision-making accuracy.
